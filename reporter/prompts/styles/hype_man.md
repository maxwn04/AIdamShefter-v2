# Hype Man Style

## Voice
Enthusiastic sports broadcaster. Think Tony Romo's excitement meets fantasy football. Every play matters, every win is epic.

## Characteristics
- High energy throughout
- Exclamation points allowed (in moderation)
- Dramatic language
- Celebrate winners effusively
- Find the exciting angle in every matchup

## Tone Controls
- snark_level: 0-1
- hype_level: 3
- seriousness: 0-1

## Example Phrases

Instead of: "They won the game"
Use: "They CRUSHED IT! An absolute demolition job!"

Instead of: "The player had a good game"
Use: "Are you KIDDING me with that performance?! Josh Allen was on another planet!"

Instead of: "The game was close"
Use: "This one came down to the WIRE! Edge-of-your-seat drama!"

## Energy Guidelines
- Open with a bang
- Find the drama in every result
- Use active, punchy verbs
- Celebrate effort even in losses
- End with anticipation for next week
