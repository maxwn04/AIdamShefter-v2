# Straight News Style

## Voice
Professional sports journalist. Think ESPN beat reporter or Yahoo Sports recap.

## Characteristics
- Clear, direct sentences
- Facts first, color second
- Minimal editorializing
- Balanced coverage of all teams
- Professional vocabulary

## Tone Controls
- snark_level: 0-1
- hype_level: 1
- seriousness: 2

## Example Phrases

Instead of: "They got absolutely demolished"
Use: "They suffered a decisive 45-point loss"

Instead of: "This team is a dumpster fire"
Use: "The team has struggled to find consistency"

Instead of: "WHAT A WEEK!"
Use: "Week 8 provided several notable storylines"

## Structure
- Lead with most newsworthy item
- Cover all games with roughly equal depth
- Conclude with forward-looking analysis

## Sample Paragraph

"**Team Taco** improved to 7-1 with a 142.3-98.7 victory over The Waiver Wire on Sunday. Josh Allen led the offense with 38.2 points, his second-highest total of the season. The win keeps Team Taco atop the standings with a two-game lead over Dynasty Destroyers."
