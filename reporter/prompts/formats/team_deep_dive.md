# Team Deep Dive Format

## Purpose
Comprehensive analysis of a single team: their identity, season arc, roster, and outlook.

## Required Sections

### 1. Team Profile (150-200 words)
- Team name and manager
- Team identity/brand
- Current record and standing
- Season narrative in brief

### 2. Season Arc (250-300 words)
- How the season has unfolded
- Key moments (best wins, worst losses)
- Turning points
- Hot and cold streaks
- Current trajectory

Use a timeline narrative:
```
The season started with promise—a 3-0 start that had the league on notice.
Then came the dark times...
```

### 3. Roster Breakdown (250-300 words)
- Core players and their contributions
- Position group analysis:
  - QB situation
  - RB room
  - WR corps
  - TE production
  - Flex flexibility
- Injury concerns
- Depth assessment

### 4. Transaction Activity (150-200 words)
- Notable trades (both directions)
- Key waiver additions
- Missed opportunities
- Roster management grade

### 5. Outlook (150-200 words)
- Playoff picture
- Remaining schedule analysis
- What needs to happen
- Bold prediction

## Tone
- Beat reporter covering their team
- Balanced but invested
- Deep knowledge of the roster
- Contextualize within the league

## Sample Opening

```markdown
# Team Taco: The Unlikely Contender

*From laughingstock to league leader—how one manager's commitment to the taco lifestyle has paid dividends*

When the season began, few gave **Team Taco** a serious look. Last year's 4-9 finish still lingered in the collective memory. Manager Mike's draft strategy—heavy on boom-or-bust receivers—raised eyebrows.

Eight weeks later, they're sitting atop the standings at 7-1, with the league's highest-scoring offense and a real shot at the championship.

This is their story.
```
